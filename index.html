Previous Next
Group Project

React | Redux | NodeJS | JavaScript | CSS | Sass | PostgreSQL | Express | Axios | BcryptJS | Amazon S3 | Google Places | Cron | Socket.io

A learning project that resembles a simple version of Podium; a workplace communication platform that brings in customer reviews as context for discussion. As part of the registration process a user would search for their business to connect the app to their Google page. Then the user would be notified within the app when a new Google review is published concerning their company after which they can discuss the review amongst the team in a chat room setting.
The entire app was built using React hooks.
